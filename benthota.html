Benthota


Bentota Turtle Hatchery is a popular conservation center located in Bentota, Sri Lanka. The hatchery is dedicated to the protection and preservation of sea turtles, which are an important part of the country's marine ecosystem. It serves as a sanctuary for injured, sick, and endangered sea turtles, providing them with a safe environment to recover and thrive.The hatchery plays a crucial role in raising awareness about the threats faced by sea turtles and educating the public about their conservation. It actively works towards protecting turtle nesting sites along the Sri Lankan coastline and promoting sustainable practices to ensure the survival of these magnificent creatures.Let's talk more about Benthota here.
